Srividya (Diya) Mohan, Ph.D.

Data Science and Bioinformatics

I am deeply passionate about developing quantitative approaches to better understand and advance the fields of diagnostics and precision medicine. I am the founder of Helixa.bio - a data science company focused on the intersection of biochemistry and machine learning. With a strong and unique background in both biology and data science and a proven track record of building impactful computational tools, I help bridge the gap between raw data and real-world impact. I am also an advisor for the California Life Sciences FAST program, through which I work with early-stage companies to develop their product and business strategies.

Please contact me through LinkedIn for consulting inquiries or collaborations.
