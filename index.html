Srividya (Diya) Mohan, Ph.D.

Data Science and Bioinformatics

I am deeply passionate about developing quantitative approaches to better understand and advance the fields of diagnostics and precision medicine. I am the founder of Helixa.bio - a data science company focused on the intersection of biochemistry and machine learning. I work with companies as a technical consultant to strategize and build bioinformatics and data analysis pipelines, ML/AI platforms, and critically evaluate projects. I am an advisor for the California Life Sciences FAST program, through which I work with early-stage companies to develop their product and business strategies.
